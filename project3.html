"A breathtaking space: Inside the exclusive bungalow of Dr. Irfan Khatri"

Dr.Irfan Khatri Villa

This luxurious villa features a blend of modern and traditional design elements, with clean lines, high ceilings, and an open-concept layout that maximizes natural light and views of the surrounding landscape. The spacious living areas are outfitted with high-end finishes and furnishings, creating a warm and inviting ambiance that's perfect for entertaining guests or simply relaxing with family.


The villa also boasts an array of outdoor living spaces, including a large terrace with stunning views . A beautifully landscaped garden that provides a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of daily life.


Our team worked closely with Mr. Irfan Khatri to bring his vision for this stunning villa to life, and we're proud to have delivered a space that exceeds his expectations in every way.
